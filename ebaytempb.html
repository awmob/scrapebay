When it comes to your canine friend, you've probably thought of all the essentials: food, flea tablets, toys, and bedding. But you probably didn't think about how you will transport your pooch from your home to the vet, for example. This is why you need to invest in a collapsible dog crate.

Types of collapsible dog crates

Just like there are many dog breeds, there are different types of collapsible dog crates to suit your budget and needs. A crate, or dog carrier, is generally a wire, stainless steel or plastic frame cage with a removable tray at the base for the dog's bedding.
